Fuzor

Fuzor is a 4 player, 2D side-scroller where players take control of four spaceship crew members. Their goal is to externimate and escape the alien infestation that has taken over their ship. During their journey, players will blast their way through enemies and combine their weapons to wreak havoc on the infestation. This game was built entirely in DigiPen's Zero Engine, a robust game engine that is actively developed in DigiPen's R&D department.
